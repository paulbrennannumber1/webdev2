Me, Myself And I. Knowing Paul Brennan.

I was born in Dublin, Ireland on the 21st of Febrary 1996. There are four in my family. Myself, my Mam, my Dad and my Brother. I am currently studying Creative Digital Media at the Institute of Technology Blanchardstown. Many people ask me what exactly is CDM? Creative Digital Media is a form of visual art done through the use of compters and creative software such as Adobe Illutrator and Photoshop. It does not require any artistic skills but one should have good visual awareness aswell as an interest in the internet and technolgy and a general understanding of how technology can be applied in creative and interesting ways.

Past Times. Hobbies.

I have a love of all thing sports, that ranges from GAA to Soccer to Rugby. I support my county Dublin in both codes of Hurling and Football. I myself play both sports for my local side. I am also an avid fan of English Premier League side Manchester United, having visited Manchester to witness many exciting games. While I don't play anymore I used to play a lot of underage Soccer, playing as a goalkeeper.

Future. Goals.

Once I complete my course and graduate I hope to go on and become a video editor. I discovered my passion for editing whilst in school trying to make XBOX videos on games such as Call of Duty. I would record gameplay with a capture device, open it in Sony Vegas 10 and then begin constructing a montage of events. As I grew older I decided to branch out and begin editing real life clips, such as snowboarding and BMX riding. I soon discovered that Introduction to Digital Media was my favourite first year module as it required students to make two short length yet high quality videos as part of an assignment.
